About The Artist

Originally from Anchorage Alaska, is an illustrator dedicated to crafting educational and narrative artworks. Specializing in wildlife art, character design, and graphic design, Audrey graduated in 2024 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts and aspires to excel in scientific illustration. Alongside pursuing her artistic passions, she engages in freelance work. Audrey finds fulfillment in collaborating with others, discussing marine biology, and exploration.
